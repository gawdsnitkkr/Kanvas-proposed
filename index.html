Photographer In You

Description
"Keep your eyes open and your mind sharp, because you don’t take a photograph, you make it". Techspardha’17 invites all those passionate about photography to participate in Kanvas-Photographer In You, your chance to be a part of the art exhibition at Techspardha 2017 and to win exciting prizes. Themes for this year's edition are:
1. Shades of Sky
2. Man and Technology

Rules
  • The contest is open for all college students.
  • Each photo must be at least 2 Megapixels in resolution and may be clicked by any camera ranging from mobile camera to a professional camera.
  • Each participant can submit max 3 number of photos relevant to the themes.
  • Each photo must have an apt caption. (It will also be considered while evaluation)
  • You are free to edit your photos, however, photos can be retouched to a certain extent as contrast, brightness etc.
  • The participant must mail the original photograph along with the retouched photograph (if any), with required information as listed below:
    • Name
    College Name
    Stream/Branch
    Year of study
  • No photograph should have watermark or any copyright.
  • Entries after 15th January 2017, 8 PM won't be considered for evaluation.
  • The result will be declared on 18th January 2017.
  • Winners will be selected from both the themes.
  • Judges will consider the creativity expressed in relevance to the theme originality and photographic ability of the photographer in capturing the photo. Judges' decision will be final.
  • By uploading/sending their photos, the participants agree to publish their photos under the Creative Commons CC BY-ND license and acknowledge that the photographs are the participants' own work. Techspardha, NIT Kurukshetra reserves the right to use the photos with due credit.
Submit your entries at kanvas@gawds.in.

Caption Writing

Description
Kanvas-Caption writing event bring you the chance to bring out that creative writer in you.
Techspardha's Facebook Page will present you a photograph daily from January 1 to 15.
You can comment caption for the pictures and best caption will win prizes and goodies.
Stay updated with Techspardha's Facebook Page.

Rules
  • The contest is open for all college students.
  • Each participant can submit max 1 number of caption per photo.
  • 15 best captions will be selected for 15 photographs posted during the time.
  • Only 1 will be adjudged winner of the Kanvas-Caption writing Event.
  • The result will be declared on 18th January 2017 .
  • It is strictly prohibited to comment anyone else's caption, this may lead to disqualification.
